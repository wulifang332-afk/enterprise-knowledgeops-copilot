# HR Leave Policy

## Purpose and Scope

This policy defines leave entitlements and approval requirements for full-time employees of Northstar Example Group. It applies globally to regular full-time employees unless a stricter local labor rule applies.

The Human Resources Operations team owns this policy and is responsible for interpretation, maintenance, and exception review.

## Annual Leave

Full-time employees receive 20 working days of annual leave per calendar year. Annual leave must be requested in the HRIS Leave module before the planned absence begins.

Annual leave requires approval from the employee's direct manager. Managers must review staffing coverage before approval.

## Sick Leave

Employees should record sick leave in the HRIS Leave module as soon as reasonably possible. Sick leave of more than 3 consecutive working days requires supporting medical documentation where permitted by local law.

Managers may approve routine sick leave entries, but Human Resources Operations may review patterns that require accommodation or policy clarification.

## Extended Leave

Leave requests longer than 10 consecutive working days are classified as extended leave. Extended leave requires direct manager review and Human Resources Operations approval before the leave begins.

If an extended leave request involves medical, family care, or statutory leave, Human Resources Operations must confirm whether local legal protections apply.

## Approval and Escalation

Employees submit leave requests in HRIS. The direct manager reviews annual leave and routine sick leave. Human Resources Operations reviews extended leave and unresolved leave disputes.

If a manager and employee disagree about leave eligibility, the case escalates to Human Resources Operations for a final policy interpretation.
