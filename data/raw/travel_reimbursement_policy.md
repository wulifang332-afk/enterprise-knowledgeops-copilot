# Travel Reimbursement Policy

## Purpose and Scope

This policy defines reimbursable business travel expenses, claim submission requirements, and approval controls. It applies to employees traveling for approved company business.

Finance Operations owns the policy and reviews exceptions with the employee's department leader when needed.

## Eligible Expenses

Eligible travel expenses include economy-class transportation, standard business lodging, ground transportation, visa fees required for business travel, and meals within the daily meal limit.

Employees must choose reasonable options and avoid expenses that would create a personal benefit unrelated to the business trip.

## Excluded Expenses

Personal entertainment, minibar charges, family travel, personal shopping, traffic fines, and upgrades not pre-approved by Finance Operations are not reimbursable.

Alcohol expenses are reimbursable only when explicitly permitted by a client event budget and approved by the department leader.

## Claim Submission

Employees must submit travel reimbursement claims in Concur Expense within 10 business days after the trip ends. Each claim must include itemized receipts and the approved business trip reference.

Receipts should be uploaded only to approved company systems. Employees must not upload receipts containing personal data to unapproved external tools.

## Approval Rules

Travel claims require approval from the employee's direct manager. Employees may not approve their own travel reimbursement claims.

Finance Operations may reject incomplete claims, claims submitted without receipts, or claims submitted after the deadline without documented justification.
