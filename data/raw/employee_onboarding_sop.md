# Employee Onboarding SOP

## Purpose and Scope

This SOP defines the standard onboarding workflow for new employees. It applies to full-time employees hired into corporate roles.

Human Resources Operations owns the onboarding process and coordinates with IT Service Management and the hiring manager.

## Pre-boarding Documents

Before onboarding begins, Human Resources Operations must collect a signed offer letter, identity verification, tax form, and emergency contact information.

The hiring manager must confirm the employee's role, start date, reporting line, and required business systems.

## HRIS Setup

Human Resources Operations creates the employee record in HRIS at least 5 business days before the start date.

The HRIS employee ID is required before IT can complete account provisioning.

## IT Account Setup

IT Service Management provisions the employee account through the Identity Management system. Standard access includes email, collaboration tools, HRIS self-service, and required security training.

Additional application access requires manager approval and the applicable system owner approval.

## Orientation and Manager Check-in

The new employee must complete orientation, security awareness training, and policy acknowledgements during the first week.

The hiring manager must complete a check-in during the first 5 business days and confirm that required equipment and system access are working.
