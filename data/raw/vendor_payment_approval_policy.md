# Vendor Payment Approval Policy

## Purpose and Scope

This policy defines approval controls for vendor payments. It applies to payments to suppliers, consultants, software providers, and other third-party vendors.

Finance Operations owns this policy and maintains the payment approval matrix.

## Required Documents

Every vendor payment request must include a Vendor Payment Request Form, a valid vendor invoice, and either an approved purchase order or an executed contract reference.

Accounts Payable may return requests that are missing required documents.

## Approval Thresholds

Vendor payments up to and including USD 10,000 require approval from the budget owner. Vendor payments above USD 10,000 and up to USD 50,000 require approval from the budget owner and Finance Manager.

Vendor payments above USD 50,000 require approval from the Finance Director and CFO before payment release.

## Exception Process

Urgent payment exceptions must be documented in the Vendor Payment Request Form and approved by Finance Operations before payment release.

Employees must not bypass approval thresholds by splitting a vendor payment into smaller requests.

## Payment Release

Accounts Payable releases approved payments through the approved banking workflow after required approvals and documents are verified.

Payments involving a new overseas vendor may require additional compliance review before release.
